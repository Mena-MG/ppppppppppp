Mathematical Rules

Newton’s Second Law

F = m × a

Description: Force is equal to mass times acceleration.

  • m = F / a
  • a = F / m

Charge Relationship

Q = I × t

Description: Charge is the product of current and time.

  • I = Q / t
  • t = Q / I

Work Done in an Electric Field

W = q × V

Description: Work done is charge multiplied by voltage.

  • q = W / V
  • V = W / q

Electric Field

F = q × E

Description: Force on a charge in an electric field.

  • q = F / E
  • E = F / q

Coulomb’s Law

F = (k × q₁ × q₂) / r²

Description: Electrostatic force between two charges.

  • q₁ = (F × r²) / (k × q₂)
  • q₂ = (F × r²) / (k × q₁)
  • r² = (k × q₁ × q₂) / F
